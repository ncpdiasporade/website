# Official July Mass Uprising Museum photographs

These optimized WebP files come from the official photo archive of the July Mass Uprising Memorial Museum. They are used as contextual, low-opacity background records on the NCP Diaspora Alliance Germany website.

The museum describes the photographs as part of the official digital archive and asks that they be used with historical context. Keep the website's visible source link and do not detach these images from their historical context.
